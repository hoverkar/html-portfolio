The Best Movies According to Karthick

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Indian

A retired freedom fighter rebels against corruption in India, which puts him in conflict with his son Chandru, who lives by corruption.

Anbe Sivam

A philosophical film with a deep message about compassion and humanity, starring Kamal Haasan.

Enthiran

Sci-fi adventure film about a scientist who creates a humanoid robot to protect humanity.
